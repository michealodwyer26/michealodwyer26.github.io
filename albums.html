Every Album I've Heard In Full

This page is a (somewhat) long list of every album I've heard in full.

Since I love listening to music, I'm often curious about what others listen to. Spotify doesn't allow others to view someone's saved music so I decided I'd list out all the albums here. I listen to a fair few genres, the majority being some type of rock. Some others for the rare occasion would be jazz, electronic, and hip-hop. There's about 195 albums (maybe give or take a couple) from around 80 different artists. Below I've made out a Top 20 albums list, which'd sort of list my favourite artists too.
