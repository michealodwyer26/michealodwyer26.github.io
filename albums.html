Every Album I've Heard In Full

This page is a long list of every album I've heard in full.

Unfortunately, Spotify doesn't allow for people to view someone else's saved music, and the few playlists I've created don't contain every album I've listened to, and are hard to browse through. So, since I have this website, I decided I'll just list them all out here, instead.

I listen to many types of music, and this list currently has 109 albums, from 44 artists, in total. The main genres are (from most prevalent to less prevalent) progressive rock, classic rock, and blues rock, jazz-fusion, avant-garde, proto-metal, blues, jazz, and a small bit of electronic, and hip-hop.

My favourite artist is Frank Zappa, who has 26 albums on this list. Other favourites (from most loved to less loved) are Jimi Hendrix, Mahavishnu Orchestra, Allan Holdsworth, Henry Cow, Kingdom Come, King Crimson, Jeff Beck, Miles Davis, E.L.P, and Otis Rush.
